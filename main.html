Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. When offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, he must perform the impossible: inception—planting an idea into someone's mind. As the team dives deeper into dream layers, reality begins to blur.

Actors
